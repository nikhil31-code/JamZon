How It Started

From a Garage in Bangalore

It started with a simple frustration: why does great audio have to cost a fortune? Our founder Rohan spent years calibrating studio gear for major labels, then decided enough was enough.

With ₹5 lakh in savings, a rented workspace in Koramangala, and three like-minded friends, JamZon was born. The first prototype took 8 months and 47 revisions.

Today we are India's fastest-growing premium audio brand, shipping to 25+ cities across the country.
